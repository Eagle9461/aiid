The following views support task-specific data exploration. These views of the database are periodically regenerated as the database develops, but they are not dynamically searching the complete set of data. If you are looking to find incidents similar to your current or fielded intelligent system, your best starting point is the [Discover](/apps/1-discover) application.

* [List of all incidents and their associated report titles](/list). View this page to track when new incidents are added.
* [Flagged incidents](/flagged). View this page to see incident reports that may have issues.
